Flight School Institute

We are a wide receiver development school that provides detail analysis of athlete’s fundamental. What makes our services unique is that we focus on the science of wide receiver performance. Flight School applies these 6 scientific methods of

  • 1) Purpose
  • 2) Research
  • 3) Hypothesis
  • 4) Experiment
  • 5) Analysis
  • 6) Conclusion

to develop proper stance, release (off and press coverage) route running leverage, creating space for the catch, catching skills which include body and hand positioning to achieve “friendly catches”, and understanding of eye dominant focus. The other unique service we offer is an ethical training component that emphasis examining your character, ownership of character, being a good teammate, and exercising wisdom. Student’s at Flight School get the total package of coaching!


About Me

I am Coach Coleman and my background is developing wide receivers into elite performers. In my playing days I was a 2 time all conference wide receiver at Weber State University and a free agent wide receiver with the Oakland Raiders. I have been in coaching for over 25 years and have a proven record of increasing receiver’s proficiency in all phases of wide receiver play. I have learned from and worked with some of the games greatest names. While coaching at the 49ers I worked under Hall of Fame legend Bill Walsh and worked with the greatest receiver of all times Hall of Famer Jerry Rice as well as Terrell (T.O.) Owens. Also, I have coached in NFL Europe as an Offensive Coordinator as well as on the college level at Sac State and Head Coach at Contra Costa College. My purpose for creating “Flight School” is two fold and that is to provide fundamental tools to wide receivers that desire to be “master craftsmen” of their position. The other reason is to provide an environment where the total person is being developed. By this I mean to teach, engage and stimulate athletes through ethical training session. My method of coaching is cerebral, and contemporary with “old school” tough love. Our 2 mantras at “Flight School” are “everyday in everyway we get a little better” and “we go after football’s like a dog going after a Frisbee!” Coach Coleman
